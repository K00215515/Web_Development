Information

VTEC or also known as Verocytotoxigenic Escherichia coli is a bacteria which can caise diarrhoea and produces a toxin which can damage your bowel wall. People generally become ill 2-4 days after infection however it can can take longer for others.

The video above will give information about VTEC and covers the following topics:

  1. What it is
  2. Sources of infection
  3. Contagious
  4. Symptoms
  5. Complications
  6. Treatment
  7. Prevention
